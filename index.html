I am Sudharssan Mohan, a Ph.D. student specializing in cybersecurity in Department of Computer Science at The University of Texas at Dallas. I am being advised by Prof. Chung Hwan Kim. My general research interest lies in system and software security. Currently, I am working on security in Autonomous Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS) especially drone security.

I completed my Bachelor's of Engineering in Computer Science and Engineering in 2021 at Rajalakshmi Engineering College in Chennai, India. I joined The University of Texas at Dallas in 2021 and started pursuing my PhD since January of 2022.

Research Interests

  • Software Security
